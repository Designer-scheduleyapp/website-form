Step 6: Email Service

What is an Email Service?
Email services let you send and receive emails using your own domain (e.g., info@yourcompany.com) in a professional way.

Options:
• Microsoft 365 – Full Suite – Includes Office apps (Word, Excel, Outlook, Teams, etc.) + Business Email
• Microsoft 365 – Email Only – Provides business email only, no apps
• AWS Email – Provides business emails only (no apps)

All options cover up to 10 users/year. Adding more users may increase cost.

Estimated Pricing:
• Microsoft 365 – Full Suite: Approx. 480 KD/year for 10 users
• Microsoft 365 – Email Only: Approx. 320 KD/year for 10 users
• AWS Email: Approx. 320 KD/year for 10 users
